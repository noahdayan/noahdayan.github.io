BIO

Noah has started practicing the saxophone at the age of 5. By the age of 12, he was awarded a music theory diploma through the conservatory and was playing several instruments including drums, piano, bass and guitar. His drumming skills were recognized through numerous awards at Berklee College of Music in Boston and as a band member during the Emergenza festival in Paris.


Noah has graduated from McGill University in Montreal with a Bachelor of Science in Honours Computer Science and Musical Applications of Technology. He currently studies Music Production and Sound Engineering at the Abbey Road Institute in London and actively produces records in the Pop and Rock genres. His latest project known as Noah's Ark showcases the best of his artistic work to date.
